About SAINT

The premier student association dedicated to fostering technical excellence and professional growth among IT students

The Largest IT Student Association

SAINT (Student Association of Information Technology) is the leading student organization in the Information Technology department, with over 500 active members and a strong alumni network spanning more than a decade.

We provide a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, develop leadership qualities, and network with industry professionals through various technical and non-technical events throughout the year.

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped our association's growth and success

2010

Founding of SAINT

SAINT was established by a group of passionate IT students with the vision to create a platform for technical and professional growth.

2013

First Tech Symposium

Organized our first annual tech symposium with 200+ attendees, featuring industry leaders from top tech companies.

2016

National Recognition

Won our first national award for "Best Student Chapter" at the National IT Conference, marking our arrival on the national stage.

2019

500+ Members

Reached the milestone of 500 active members, becoming the largest student association in the IT department.

2022

Hackathon Champions

Our team won the National Collegiate Hackathon, competing against 50+ teams with an innovative AI solution.
